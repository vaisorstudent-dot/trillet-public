Common questions

Pricing questions we hear most.

"What's included on every plan?"
Full white-label, full markup, unlimited concurrency, and operational API. The core platform is the same across all three tiers. You never pay to unlock features you need from day one.
"What actually changes between tiers?"
Sub-account count (3 / 10 / Unlimited), commercial automation (self-signup, SaaS mode on Scale), and support closeness (email -> priority -> named partner). That's it.
"Does per-minute pricing change by tier?"
No. $0.12/min across all agency tiers. Scale-tier agencies with high volume can have a conversation about volume rates, but there's no built-in tier markup on usage.
"What if I just need it for my own business?"
There's a separate individual plan at $49/mo for businesses that want a voice AI receptionist without the agency tooling. Different path, same underlying platform.
"Is there a free trial?"
Yes. Start on Entry with a free trial period. Deploy an agent, test it with real calls, and see the platform before committing. No credit card until you're ready to go live with clients.
"Can I upgrade mid-month?"
Yes, instantly. If you sign a 4th client on Entry, upgrade to Core and the new sub-account limit applies immediately. Prorated billing, no disruption.
