Common questions

Pricing questions we hear most.

"What's included on every plan?"
Full white-label, full markup control, full API access, and agent templates. The core platform is the same across all three tiers. You're not paying to unlock features you need from day one.
"What actually changes between tiers?"
Workspace count (5 hard cap / 10 with overage / 15 with overage), Scale-only features (SaaS configurator, custom agents, BYOK), support closeness (email+chatbot / priority / named partner), and per-minute rate ($0.12 / $0.12 / $0.10). That's it.
"Does per-minute pricing change by tier?"
Starter and Agency are both $0.12/min flat. Scale gets $0.10/min as a volume reward.
"What if I outgrow my workspace count?"
Agency and Scale have overage at +$20/workspace. Starter is a hard cap at 5 -- once you need a 6th workspace, the trigger to upgrade to Agency is clear. No disruption, instant upgrade.
"What if I just need it for my own business?"
There's a separate individual plan at $49/mo for businesses that want a voice AI receptionist without the agency tooling. Different path, same underlying platform.
"Can I upgrade mid-month?"
Yes, instantly. Hit your workspace cap on Starter, upgrade to Agency and the new limit applies immediately. Prorated billing, no disruption to active agents.
