How it works

Three steps. Your phone is covered.

Step 1
Give it your website
The AI reads your site and learns your services, hours, pricing, and FAQs. No manual scripting. If your website describes your business, the agent already knows it.
Step 2
Forward your calls
Set up call forwarding from your existing business number. When you're busy, in a meeting, or closed for the day, calls go to your AI receptionist instead of voicemail.
Step 3
Get summaries, not voicemails
Every call is summarized with the caller's name, number, reason for calling, and any appointments booked. You see what happened, not a garbled voicemail you'll never listen to.
