Key Finding

The $99 Studio tier is not a funnel to $299. It is a separate population that either stays at $99 or churns. Not a single Studio customer has ever upgraded to AgencyPlus. Studio customers churn at a higher rate (28% vs 25%) while generating less than half the revenue. At $149, you filter out the weakest cohort without losing any Agency pipeline -- because Studio never fed Agency in the first place.

From the Community Manager (Andrew)

Andrew confirms the Studio cohort splits into two halves:

~50% complete newbies: "They are horrific. They have never used ChatGPT even." Expected to either not complete their 7-day trial or churn within a month.

~50% have technical know-how but can't sell. Expected to churn within 3 months.

Neither half is on a path to becoming a serious agency. The $99 tier is attracting people who will never generate per-minute revenue.
