How deployment works

From new client to live agent in one sitting.

1
Paste the client's website URL
The AI reads services, hours, pricing, FAQs, and location. No manual data entry. A dental practice with a website gets a dental agent that already knows their insurance list and office hours.
2
Adjust what matters
Change the greeting, add specific routing rules, configure booking behavior, set business hours. The industry logic is already there -- you're tuning it for this specific client, not building from scratch.
3
Forward their number. Done.
Client sets up conditional forwarding to the Trillet number. Their existing phone number stays the same. Callers don't notice anything except that someone always answers. You bill them through Stripe.
