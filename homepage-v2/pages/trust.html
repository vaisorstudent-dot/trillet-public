Compliance posture.

What is live today, what is in active build, and what we do not yet assert. Same posture in due-diligence as on this page.

Download as PDF

Live today

On-premise Docker deployment
Trillet deploys inside your environment via Docker. The voice agent processes and stores data within your own infrastructure. Verizon TCC precedent.
Private-cloud deployment
Dedicated tenant in your AWS / GCP / Azure account. You own the compute boundary. Configurable VPC + IAM integration.
Configurable data residency
Data stays in the geography your environment requires. Deployment-time configuration, not a per-call decision.
Encryption in transit and at rest
Standard cipher suites end-to-end. Key management options per deployment.
Audit trail and observability
Every call recorded, transcribed, scored, and logged to your retention policy. Searchable audit log with role-scoped access.

Deployment + data residency

Where the data lives.

On-premise Docker

Data stays inside your infrastructure. No external egress required. You control retention, access, audit.

Private cloud (your account)

Dedicated tenant in your AWS / GCP / Azure account. You own the compute + storage boundary. Trillet-managed deployment, customer-owned infrastructure.

Managed enterprise (Trillet-hosted)

Trillet-managed deployment with configurable data residency by geography. US / EU / UK / AU / regional configurable per region.

Audit + observability

Every call. Auditable.

  • Per-call recording, transcript, scoring, and structured event log.
  • Retention configurable per deployment per regulatory requirement.
  • Role-scoped access to audit logs (admin / supervisor / agent / auditor roles).
  • Independent audit access available on engagement — coordinate with your selected third-party assessor.

Security incidents

Incident response posture.

We do not currently assert a financially-guaranteed uptime SLA on custom-agent deployments. Enterprise-tier SLA available under contract on request, scope per engagement. Security incident response process below applies to all deployments.

01 · Detection

Continuous observability

Continuous metrics across the call path. Alerting on call-failure thresholds, latency anomalies, sub-processor errors.

02 · Notification

Customer notification

Customer notification per agreement (timeline configurable per deployment per regulatory requirement). Default: same-business-day on customer-impacting incidents.

03 · Resolution

Resolution + post-mortem

Resolution per severity. Post-mortem written and shared with customer for any P1 incident. Process improvements tracked.
