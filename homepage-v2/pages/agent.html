What it does · § 01

Four jobs, one call.

Every call runs through the same four jobs that power enterprise deployments — scoped to your business.

01

Verify

Identity-aware from the first second. The agent knows who's calling before any account-touching action. Configurable per-call scope.

02

Ground

Reads your calendar, business info, and prior context live during the call. Not a static script — an agent with your live business context in hand.

03

Act

Routes the call, books, confirms, summarizes, updates your records. Every action authorized and scoped to what you permit.

04

Follow through

After the call wraps: email summary to the owner, calendar update, CRM write, alert on anything flagged. The work keeps moving without you.
