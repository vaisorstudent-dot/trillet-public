Pricing questions.

What does white-label actually mean?

Brand surface (logo, colors, voice persona, agent name, transcript styling, call-summary email template), custom domain or subdomain per client, and the operator console under your agency brand. Your name on every customer-facing surface, not Trillet's.

Can I deploy a client without buying a tier upgrade first?

Yes — Starter tier supports 1 client deployment. Agency tier opens multi-client. Scale tier opens unlimited.

How does per-minute voice pricing work? Am I billed per call or in aggregate?

Top up your account balance; calls draw from it as they happen. Per-minute rate is $0.12/min standard, $0.18/min for ElevenLabs voices. Aggregate billing per period — no per-call invoices.

Can I switch tiers mid-month?

Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately, prorated. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

Do you offer annual billing?

Annual billing is available on Agency and Scale tiers — contact us for current discount and terms.

Is there a free trial?

Yes — 7-day free trial on all tiers. Try risk-free with full access to all features.

Can I bring my own voice model / vendor?

Scale tier supports BYOK (Bring Your Own Keys) for LLM, STT, and TTS. Lower tiers use Trillet-managed voice infrastructure.
