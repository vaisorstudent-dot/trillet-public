§ 01  ·  The spine

A call, documented from first ring to close.

Every call runs the same four-phase spine, and every step leaves a record. The agent only says what it can prove — and only acts inside a policy your team wrote. The call ends when the work is done, not when the caller hangs up.

Phase 01
VerifyKnow who's calling.
ANI lookup, DOB or PIN challenge, voice biometrics on the second call. Failed attempts route to a human or get escalated before any patient or customer data is shared. Identity is a precondition, not a feature.
Log · phase 01 ani.lookup → known dob.match → confirmed session: established
Phase 02
GroundPull real context.
Every turn is grounded against your systems of record — EHR, CRM, calendar, ticketing, POS, whatever holds the truth. The agent only says what it can prove. Hallucinations are a policy failure, not a model failure.
Log · phase 02 ehr.patient.get(8102) cal.availability(chen) policy.intake.lookup
Phase 03
ActDo it, don't just talk.
The agent writes back to the same systems it read from — books, reschedules, refunds, opens tickets, triggers downstream workflows. Every action is reversible, logged, and policy-gated. The caller hangs up with their problem solved.
Log · phase 03 cal.move(44812, tue_14:30) sms.confirm → sent policy.check → ok
Phase 04
FollowClose the loop.
Confirmations sent. Summary written to your CRM. Downstream workflows kicked off. Anything the QA rubric flagged goes to a human reviewer. The call ends when the work is done, not when the phone hangs up.
Log · phase 04 summary.post(hubspot) email.confirm → sent qa.flagged → reviewed
