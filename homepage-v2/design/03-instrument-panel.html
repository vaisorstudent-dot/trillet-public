§ 02 · POSITION

Most voice agents are an IVR with a voice.

They route, transcribe, maybe answer an FAQ — then hand off. Trillet finishes the job inside your systems so no one has to call back tomorrow to actually fix it.

CH_AMOST_VOICE_AGENTS
They sound human. Then they stop.
  • [i]Route the call, maybe answer an FAQ from a static knowledge base.
  • [ii]Take a message, email the team, ask a human to follow up tomorrow.
  • [iii]No integration to your systems of record — every answer is a guess.
  • [iv]No action during the call — caller hangs up without their problem solved.
  • [v]The transcript is the product. Someone else still has to do the work.
CH_BTRILLET
We finish the job, inside your systems.
  • [i]Ground every turn against your EHR, CRM, calendar, or ticketing.
  • [ii]Write back — book, reschedule, refund, open tickets, trigger workflows.
  • [iii]Escalate to a human only when policy requires — with full context attached.
  • [iv]Follow through after hangup — confirms, summaries, workflows, QA flags.
  • [v]Every action logged, reversible, policy-gated. The call ends when the work is done.
