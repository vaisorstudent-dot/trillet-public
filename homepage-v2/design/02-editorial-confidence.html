§ Colophon · The four-phase spine

How every call actually runs.

One shape across every industry we serve — clinics, dealerships, field service, agencies. A call is a workflow, and a workflow has phases. Trillet runs all four; most agents stop at two.

01
VerifyKnow who's calling.
ANI lookup, DOB or PIN challenge, voice biometrics on the second call. Failed attempts route to a human or get escalated before any patient or customer data is shared. This is not a feature — it's a precondition.
Observedani.lookup → known_patient · dob.match → confirmed · session.established
02
GroundPull real context.
Every turn is grounded against your systems of record — EHR, CRM, calendar, ticketing, POS, whatever holds the truth. The agent only says what it can prove from your source of truth. Hallucinations are a policy failure, not a model failure.
Observedehr.patient.get(8102) · cal.availability(chen) · policy.intake.lookup
03
ActDo it, don't just talk.
The agent writes back to the same systems it read from — books, reschedules, refunds, opens tickets, triggers downstream workflows. Actions are reversible, logged, and policy-gated. The caller hangs up with their problem solved, not with a promise that someone will call back.
Observedcal.move(44812, tue_2_30) · notify.sms · policy.check → ok
04
FollowClose the loop after hangup.
Confirmations sent. Summary written to your CRM. Downstream workflows kicked off. Flags raised for anything the QA rubric caught. All of it before anyone has to ask. The call doesn't end at "goodbye" — it ends when the work is done.
Observedsummary.post(hubspot) · confirm.email · qa.flagged → reviewed
§ Position

Most voice agents are an IVR with a voice.

MOST VOICE AGENTS

They sound human. Then they stop.

  • i.Route the call, maybe answer an FAQ from a static knowledge base.
  • ii.Take a message, email the team, ask a human to follow up tomorrow morning.
  • iii.No integration to your systems of record — so every answer is a best guess.
  • iv.No action taken during the call — the caller hangs up without their problem solved.
  • v.The transcript is the product. Someone else has to do the work.
TRILLET

We finish the job, inside your systems.

  • i.Ground every turn against your EHR, CRM, calendar, or ticketing — never a static FAQ.
  • ii.Write back to the same systems — book, reschedule, refund, open tickets, trigger workflows.
  • iii.Escalate to a human only when policy requires it — and with the full context, not a cold handoff.
  • iv.Follow through after hangup — confirmations, summaries, downstream workflows, QA flags.
  • v.Every action logged, reversible, policy-gated. The call ends when the work is done.
