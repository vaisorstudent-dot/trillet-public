05 — Why This Architecture

Why we built it this way.

a. Markup is the business model

An agency that can’t mark up isn’t an agency, it’s a reseller. Every competitor gives 100% of markup from entry. Gating it is like selling a franchise but not letting them set menu prices.

b. Sub-accounts are a sufficient upgrade trigger

You can’t serve 10 clients on 3 sub-accounts. Growth forces the upgrade. The best outcome: agencies upgrade because they’re growing, not because we put up a wall.

c. Full SaaS mode is the right commercial gate

At Scale, agencies get the complete package: clients self-signup, rebilling with markup, client-facing billing. The jump from “running an agency” to “running a software business.” One meaningful gate, not seven arbitrary ones.

d. Per-minute economics favor open access

10-client agency: 10K min/mo = $1,200 per-minute revenue vs $299 subscription. Feature gates that slow adoption cost 4x what they gain in subscription premium.
