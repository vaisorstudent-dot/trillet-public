FAQ

Common questions.

Can I upgrade between plans?

Yes. Move from Starter to Agency to Scale at any time. Your agents, clients, and configuration carry over. Downgrading works too — no lock-in.

What are usage costs on top of the base price?

You pay per minute of AI call time. D2C Starter is $0.20/min. Starter and Agency plans are $0.12/min flat. Scale plan is $0.10/min. Agency plans can mark up usage and rebill to clients.

Do clients see the Trillet brand?

Not on Starter, Agency, or Scale plans. Full white-label means your brand, your domain, your login. Clients interact only with your brand.

How does rebilling work?

Set your own price per client. Connect your Stripe. Trillet bills your clients automatically under your brand. You keep 100% of the spread above your Trillet cost.

What's the difference between Agency and Scale?

Scale adds 5 more workspaces, a volume per-minute discount ($0.10 vs $0.12), the SaaS configurator for faster client setup, custom agent builds, BYOK, and a named partner for dedicated support. If you're managing more than 10 clients or want custom-built agents, Scale is the right move.

What is BYOK and who needs it?

BYOK (bring your own keys) lets Scale plan operators connect their own LLM and telephony API credentials. This gives you direct cost control and model flexibility — useful for high-volume operators or those with existing provider contracts.
